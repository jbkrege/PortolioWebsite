Projects

Melody Maker is designed to bridge the gap between growing AI technology and creatives. Musicians use its web interface to write music in a feedback cycle with a generative neural network trained with various genres of music. Built using TensorFlow and Magenta.js and hosted on Firebase. This project was supported by Northwestern's Undergraduate Research Grant in the fall of 2018.
MusiqueMan explores the ability of neural nets to capture compositional style by training a generative network based on Daniel Johnson's novel biaxial LSTM architecture with Bach, Chopin, and Beatles-like music. (2016)
The Similarity Matrix Granulator, or SMG, is a granular synthesizer with an extra dimension – the ability to specify the similarity of grains to further sculpt sounds. It currently exists only as a Python CLI tool available on Github, but I’m planning to make a real-time version with Juce or Max MSP. (2016)
The laser harp, Orpheus, embodies a dream of what electronic music performance could be. (2018)
Marcia Fraerman's website was a freelance project I took on to grow my web/design skills. Progressive single page web app made with HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and Javascript, with scripts allowing her to upload more photos. Hosted on Firebase. (Summer 2018)
